## Advanced Research Guidelines

### 1. AI Opportunity Detection (Critical)
Look for these HIGH-VALUE automation opportunities:

**Customer Service Automation ($100K-500K opportunity)**
- High support volume, repetitive inquiries
- Multiple communication channels
- Customer onboarding complexity
- Technical documentation needs

**Data Processing & Analysis ($75K-300K opportunity)**  
- Manual data entry/processing workflows
- Report generation taking significant time
- Cross-platform data integration needs
- Compliance reporting requirements

**Sales & Marketing Automation ($50K-200K opportunity)**
- Lead qualification processes
- Personalized content generation at scale
- CRM data enrichment needs
- Sales process inefficiencies

**Research & Intelligence ($60K-250K opportunity)**
- Market research conducted manually
- Competitive intelligence gathering
- Due diligence processes
- Knowledge management challenges

### 2. Decision Maker Identification
**Primary Budget Holders:**
- CTO, VP Engineering (technical feasibility)
- COO, VP Operations (process improvement)  
- CEO (strategic transformation)
- Chief Digital Officer (digital initiatives)

**Secondary Influencers:**
- VP Sales (revenue impact)
- CFO (ROI justification) 
- VP Customer Success (service improvement)
- Director of IT (implementation)

### 3. Company Size & Budget Estimation
Use these signals:
- **Enterprise (1000+):** Multiple locations, complex org structure, dedicated IT/AI teams
- **Mid-Market (200-1000):** Regional presence, growing pains, some automation already
- **SMB (50-200):** Single/few locations, manual processes, cost-conscious
- **Startup (<50):** Rapid growth, tech-forward, limited resources

### 4. Pain Point Qualification
Rate each pain point by:
- **Frequency:** How often does this problem occur?
- **Cost:** What's the current cost of this inefficiency?  
- **AI Suitability:** Can custom agents solve this effectively?
- **ROI Timeline:** Payback period under 12 months?

**Healthcare/Medical:**

**Professional Services:**
- Client onboarding automation

**Financial Services:**

**E-commerce/Retail:**

**SaaS/Technology:**

### 6. Competitive Intelligence
Identify if they're already:
- Using basic automation (Zapier, IFTTT)
- Working with other AI vendors
- Built internal automation teams
